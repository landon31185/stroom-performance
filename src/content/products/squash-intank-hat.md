The intank hat is where a serious fuel system starts. At altitude the engine
needs more fuel volume to make the same power it would at sea level, and a
proper hat lets you fit the pump(s) to deliver it without a mess of external
plumbing.

Pair it with the trap-door access mod so you can service the pump without
dropping the tank every time.
