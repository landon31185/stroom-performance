Dropping a fuel tank to service a pump is miserable — and at altitude you'll be
in the fuel system more than most, because you're pushing volume. A trap door
turns a full-day job into a quick access panel.

This is an advanced fabrication job. Measure twice, cut once, and keep the
"Death Wheel" away from anything you don't want to breach.
